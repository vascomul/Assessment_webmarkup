Haarlem Center

Adem Haarlem

Main Content

Haarlem Center, one of the biggest cities in the Netherlands. The city is right next to the Spaarne. Haarlem is also part of the Randstad, the Randstad is a region with a couple cities in it, among which: Amsterdam, Rotterdam, Den Haag, Utrecht and Haarlem. Spaarndam, a place close to Haarlem, belongs to Haarlem Township. The city has over 150.000 inhabitants and with a large arrangement for shopping and places to eat, it is one of the most visited places in the Netherlands. Haarlem is number 6 on the monument city list and that’s because the city goes centuries back in time with buildings build a long time ago, and there are 1.114 different buildings! And for the ones who love to walk with their pets, or just for the ones who love nature, there is a huge park called “Haarlemmerhout stadspark” which is in the center of Haarlem.

Secondary Content

In the center of Haarlem are many places where you can walk for hours with friends and family. For the big friend groups, there are shops like: Number Nine, ID, Kloffie, and many more! One of the biggest shops in Haarlem is the Apple store, this is the place to be when you want to buy new products from this big brand. Think about the new Airpods pro, or the Iphone 11 Pro Max, products you don’t want to miss, or maybe want to try! For the families who want to dine with each other, the city offers a lot of places where you can sit down and catch up with each other. In the middle of the center, there is a square where you can sit down and relax with a cup of coffee, something to eat, and when its time for dinner, there are even more places to eat, every restaurant opens their doors and welcome you to eat everything you adore! For the people who want to live in this beautiful city, there are a couple upcoming places, such as: Parkwijk, Sinnevelt, Delftwijk, Van Zeggelenbuurt, Molenwijk. These places are not to expensive to live, and in these places are a lot of places to eat and a lot of supermarkets to get your groceries. People from outside Haarlem are also welcome in this city. For the tourists there are many museums to go, churches, and more typical places in Haarlem. A couple times a year, there is a market on the biggest square of Haarlem center. On this market many people sell their products, and there are little stalls where people make typical food from their country of origin. In short, there are many places to go when you’re in Haarlem, or when you are on vacation in the Netherlands, so feel free to visit this beautiful place in the Netherlands.

Sport Content

Taekwondo is one of the most popular self-defense sports in the world. Its not only fighting, it is a way of thinking, discipline and trust in yourself. (Click the pictures to go to the websites!)

  • Sportcity Haarlem is a place where people can do fitness, where people can be coached by professionals and where everyone, no matter which age, is welcome.

  • PannaKnockout is a center where people play soccer against each other, in a little "stadium" with not more than 3 players.

  • Scopo Atletico is a club where they want people to move, by organizing different types of sports workshops.

  • Activae helps people reach their fitness goals by giving the customers eating schedules, and they help you with your mindset, to optimize the road to your goal.

  • Koninklijke Nederlandse Schaakbond is a place where you can find peace with chess. At this sportsclub you can get personal coaching for getting better at the sport of you can sign up to be a chess coach yourself.

  • Zelfverdediging "In de Toelast" is a sportsclub where self defense is more than only the training, It is also a place where joy is very important.

  • Sportsclub Kenamju is an open gym for everyone, from top athletes to the most inexperienced people, everyone is more than welcome.

  • Olympia Haarlem is a place where many sports can be done, for example: Soccer, Softball and Billiards. They also offer people the chance to hire a room for private parties, etc.

  • HWA-Rang Dragon has different kind of sports, among which Taekwondo. When you sign up for this sportsclub, they help you with your way to perfection of the sport. They support every experience group.

  • Personal Training Group is a club where people get help during their training session, and the customers get eating schedules from the Personal Trainers. This sportsclub has no website.
